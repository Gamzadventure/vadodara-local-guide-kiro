# Vadodara Local City Cultural & Food Guide

This context file teaches Kiro about Vadodara city so it can provide culturally accurate, locally relevant responses.

## City Overview
Vadodara, also known as Baroda, is a cultural city in Gujarat, India. It is famous for education, heritage, and food.

## Languages
- Gujarati is the primary local language
- Hindi and English are widely understood
- Polite and respectful tone is preferred

## Local Food Culture
Popular street foods and dishes:
- Sev Usal
- Fafda Jalebi
- Kachori
- Dabeli
- Gujarati Thali

Popular food areas:
- Alkapuri
- Sayajiganj
- Manjalpur
- Fatehgunj

## Cultural Habits
- Shops often close between 2 PM and 4 PM
- People are family-oriented and community-focused
- Festivals like Navratri, Uttarayan, and Diwali are celebrated widely

## Travel & Daily Life Tips
- Auto rickshaws and city buses are common transport
- Peak traffic hours: 9–11 AM and 6–8 PM
- Summers are very hot; evenings are preferred for outings

## Behavior Guidelines
- Respect elders and local customs
- Vegetarian food is widely preferred
- Modest clothing is appreciated in traditional areas
